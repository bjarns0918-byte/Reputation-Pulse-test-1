FAQ

Questions people actually ask.

Do I need to be technical to use this?

No. You connect your Google Business Profile once, and everything after that runs automatically in the background.

Will it post something embarrassing without me knowing?

Only straightforwardly positive reviews (4-5 stars) get auto-posted. Anything more sensitive (1-3 stars) waits in your dashboard for your approval first - it never posts without your say-so.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, directly from your account settings - no phone calls, no emails required. You keep access through the end of your current billing period.

Does this work with Yelp or Facebook too?

Right now, Reputation Pulse focuses entirely on Google, since it's where most restaurants get reviewed and where the deepest integration is possible.

What happens after the free trial?

You're charged automatically at the end of 7 days, based on the plan you chose. You can cancel before then and you won't be charged at all.
