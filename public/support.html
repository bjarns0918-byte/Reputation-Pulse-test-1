Support & FAQ

Common questions, answered plainly.

What happens if I don't like a drafted reply?

You're never stuck with what we draft. For 1-3 star reviews, you'll see two reply options and can edit either one directly in your dashboard before posting - change a word, rewrite a sentence, or the whole thing. Nothing posts without your approval.

Can I switch between the monthly and annual plan?

Yes. Reach out to us at repanchorapp@gmail.com and we'll take care of the switch for you.

Is my data safe?

Your password is never stored in plain text - only a secure one-way hash. Your Google connection is used solely to read and reply to your own reviews. We don't sell your data or share it with advertisers.

What if I miss a review that needed a personal touch?

4 and 5-star reviews post automatically. Anything more sensitive (1-3 stars) always waits for your approval first, and you'll get an instant email alert the moment one comes in - not just in the monthly digest.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, directly from your account settings - no phone calls needed. You'll keep access through the end of your current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Yes - if you're not satisfied, email us at repanchorapp@gmail.com within 24 hours of being charged for a full refund. Requests after that window are considered case by case.

Do you support Yelp or Facebook reviews?

Not yet - we're focused entirely on Google right now, since it's where the deepest, most reliable integration is possible.
